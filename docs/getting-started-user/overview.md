# Getting Started - User Guide

Welcome to Almena ID! This guide will help you get started with your decentralized identity wallet.

## What is Almena ID?

Almena ID is a decentralized identity platform that gives you complete control over your digital identity. Your identity is secured by cryptography and only you have access to it.

## Prerequisites

- Almena ID wallet installed on your device

See [Platform Compatibility](../user-guide/browser-compatibility.md) for detailed requirements.

## Installation

### Desktop (Windows, macOS, Linux)

1. Download the Almena ID installer from the official website
2. Run the installer for your operating system
3. Follow the installation wizard
4. Launch Almena ID from your applications

### Mobile (Android, iOS)

1. Visit your app store (Google Play or App Store)
2. Search for "Almena ID"
3. Install the application
4. Open the app

See [Platform Compatibility](../user-guide/browser-compatibility.md) for detailed installation instructions.

### Web Application

Visit [almena.id](https://almena.id) (or your deployment URL) to access the web interface.

## First Time Setup

When you first open Almena ID, you'll see two options:

### Option 1: Create New Account

If you're new to Almena ID:

1. Click **"Create New Account"**
2. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters with letters, numbers, and special characters)
3. **IMPORTANT**: You'll receive a 12-word recovery phrase
4. Write down your recovery phrase and store it safely
5. Your identity will be created and you'll see your dashboard

**⚠️ Critical**: Never share your recovery phrase with anyone. Anyone with your recovery phrase can access your identity.

### Option 2: Recover Account

If you already have an Almena ID account:

1. Click **"Recover Account"**
2. Enter your 12-word recovery phrase
3. Create a new password for this device
4. Your identity will be restored

## Your Identity

Once set up, you'll have:

- **DID (Decentralized Identifier)**: Your unique identity on the platform (format: `did:almena:...`)
- **Public Key**: Your public cryptographic key for verification
- **Private Key**: Encrypted and stored securely on your device

## Security Notes

- **Your password** never leaves your device
- **Your private key** is encrypted with your password
- **Your recovery phrase** is the ONLY way to recover your account
- **We cannot recover** your account if you lose your recovery phrase
