# Dashboard

Your dashboard is the main screen in Almena ID where you can view and manage your decentralized identity.

## Dashboard Overview

When you open Almena ID after logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:

### Your Identity Information

**DID (Decentralized Identifier)**
- Your unique identity address
- Format: `did:almena:` followed by a unique hash
- Click the copy icon to copy to clipboard
- Share this with others to let them verify your identity

**Public Key**
- Your cryptographic public key
- Used for verification and encryption
- Safe to share publicly
- Click the copy icon to copy to clipboard

### Navigation Menu

Access different sections:
- **Home**: Your main dashboard (current page)
- **Identity**: Manage your identity details (coming soon)
- **Credentials**: View and manage credentials (coming soon)
- **Security**: Security settings (coming soon)
- **Settings**: Configure preferences

## Using Your Dashboard

### Copying Your DID

1. Locate your DID on the dashboard
2. Click the copy icon next to your DID
3. Your DID is now in your clipboard
4. Paste it wherever needed

### Copying Your Public Key

1. Find your Public Key below your DID
2. Click the copy icon
3. The key is copied to your clipboard

### Sharing Your Identity

Your DID is safe to share publicly. Use it when:
- Someone needs to verify your identity
- Registering with services that support DIDs
- Receiving verifiable credentials
- Connecting with other users

**Remember**: Only share your DID and Public Key, **never** your password or recovery phrase.

## Quick Actions

### Settings
Click the settings icon to:
- Change your language
- View account information
- Configure preferences

### Logout
To logout securely:
1. Click the profile or menu icon
2. Select "Logout"
3. Confirm your choice

**Note**: Logging out clears your session but your encrypted data remains safe in local storage.
